# The Tech lead label
*Build systems, not software*

The tech lead is more of label than a role. It alters how you spend your time but has the same expectations associated with your level. You will engage with the team differently.

### What you will do

1. guide informed decisions on the team
2. identify technical risk
3. provide a strong technical direction involves establishing a technical vision
4. spend about 30% of their time in the code
5. provide architectural guidance 
6. unblock the team
7. leverage your technical prowess to make the team more effective
8. giving insightful feedback about delivery
9. stay out of the critical path of delivery, usually
